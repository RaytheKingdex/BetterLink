1 Getting Started

BetterLink is a career networking platform that connects students with employment opportunities and employers with emerging talent. Whether you're a student looking for your first job or an employer searching for qualified candidates, BetterLink has the tools to help you succeed.

Who Is BetterLink For?

  • Students & Job Seekers — Browse jobs, build your profile, follow communities, and connect with employers.
  • Employers & Recruiters — Post job listings, review applications, and message candidates directly.
Tip: You can access BetterLink from any modern web browser — no installation required.

2 Creating an Account

To use BetterLink's full features, you need to create a free account.

  • 1 Navigate to the BetterLink homepage and click Sign Up.
  • 2 Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password.
  • 3 Select your account type: Student or Employer.
  • 4 Click Create Account to complete registration.
  • 5 You will be redirected to your Feed once registration is successful.
Note: Use a valid email address — it will be used for account recovery and notifications.

3 Logging In

  • 1 Click Login in the navigation bar.
  • 2 Enter your registered email address and password.
  • 3 Click Sign In.
  • 4 You will be redirected to your personal Feed.
If you're already logged in, visiting the Home page will automatically redirect you to your Feed.

Signing Out

Click the Sign Out button in the top navigation bar of any app page to securely log out of your session.

4 Your Profile

Your profile is your professional identity on BetterLink. Keep it up to date to improve your visibility with employers.

Viewing Your Profile

Click Profile in the sidebar navigation to view and edit your profile page.

Profile Information

  • Display Name — Your name as shown to other users.
  • Bio / About — A short description of yourself, your skills, or your goals.
  • Profile Photo — Upload a professional photo to make your profile stand out.
  • Followers / Following — See who follows you and who you follow on the platform.
Profiles with photos and complete bios are more likely to be noticed by employers.

5 The Feed

The Feed is your home after logging in. It shows posts from people you follow and activity across the platform.

Creating a Post

  • 1 Click inside the compose box at the top of the Feed.
  • 2 Type your message or update.
  • 3 Optionally, click Attach to add an image or video.
  • 4 Click Post to publish.

Interacting with Posts

  • Like — Click the heart/like button on a post to show appreciation.
  • Comment — Click the comment icon to leave a reply.
  • Delete — You can delete your own posts at any time.

Following Users

Visit a user's profile and click Follow to see their posts in your Feed. You can unfollow them the same way.

6 Jobs & Opportunities

The Jobs section lists all active job postings on BetterLink, helping students find relevant opportunities quickly.

Browsing Jobs

  • 1 Click Jobs in the sidebar navigation.
  • 2 Browse the list of available positions.
  • 3 Click on a job listing to see its full details.

Applying for a Job

  • 1 Open a job listing.
  • 2 Review the job description, requirements, and employer details.
  • 3 Click Apply to submit your application.
Complete your profile before applying — employers can view your profile as part of your application.

7 My Jobs (Employer)

Employers can manage their job listings and review applicants from the My Jobs dashboard.

Posting a Job

  • 1 Navigate to My Jobs in the sidebar.
  • 2 Click Post a Job.
  • 3 Fill in the job title, description, requirements, and other details.
  • 4 Click Submit to publish the listing.

Managing Applications

  • View all applicants for each of your job listings.
  • Hire a candidate by clicking the Hire button on their application.
  • Hired candidates automatically receive an onboarding notification.

Closing a Job

Once a position is filled, you can delete or close the listing from the My Jobs page to stop receiving new applications.

8 Communities

Communities let you connect with groups of people sharing similar interests, industries, or goals.

Browsing Communities

Click Communities in the sidebar to see available groups.

Joining a Community

  • 1 Find a community that interests you.
  • 2 Click Join.
  • 3 You will see posts from that community in its dedicated feed.

Creating a Community

  • 1 Click Create Community.
  • 2 Enter a community name and description.
  • 3 Click Create to launch the community.

9 Account & Settings

Changing Your Password

Navigate to your Profile settings and update your password. Always use a strong, unique password.

Privacy

Your email address is never publicly shown to other users. Only your display name and profile content are visible.

Notifications

BetterLink may notify you of new followers, job application updates, and messages. Check your email for platform notifications.

Never share your password with anyone. BetterLink staff will never ask for your password.

10 Troubleshooting & Support

I can't log in

Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. If the issue persists, contact support.

My post didn't appear

Refresh the page. If the post is still missing, try reposting. Make sure your text is not empty.

I can't see any jobs

Ensure you are logged in. If jobs still don't appear, the page may still be loading — wait a moment and refresh.

Contact Us

For further assistance, visit our Contact Us page or email support at support@betterlink.com.

Include as much detail as possible when contacting support — screenshots and error messages are very helpful.
